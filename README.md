# Empowering Nuer Women

**Digital Transparency & Humanitarian Action Hub in South Sudan**

## About

Empowering Nuer Women is a women-led initiative committed to transparency, accountability, and sustainable development in South Sudan. This website serves as a digital platform to connect donors, partners, and communities.

## Website Pages

- Home - Overview of our mission and work
- About - Our story, values, and leadership team
- Our Work - Education, healthcare, livelihoods, and transparency programs
- Impact Stories - Real stories of transformation
- Volunteer - Join our volunteer team
- Contact - Get in touch with us
- Donate - Support our mission
